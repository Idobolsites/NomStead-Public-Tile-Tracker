# NomStead-Public-Tile-Tracker
# 🌾 NomStead Public Tile Tracker  A lightweight, community-driven web utility designed to track publicly shared tiles in **NomStead**. This tool helps players optimize their farming routes, manage cooldowns, and collaborate efficiently without any server-side clutter.

## 🚀 Features

* **Verified Community Registry:** Filter and view public tiles categorized by biomes (Farmland, Forest, Pond, Mountain, Gold Furnace).
* **Custom Farmland Timers:** Set individual hour/minute countdowns for your crops. Boxes automatically jump to the top and turn green when they are `🌾 Ready to harvest!`.
* **Pond Cooldown Checklist:** Easily mark visited ponds as empty. 
* **Global Bi-Daily Resets:** Integrated automatic client-side reset at **0:00 UTC** and **12:00 UTC** to align perfectly with global game-world respawns.
* **100% Serverless & Private:** Built with pure HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. All your active timers and checkmarks are saved securely on your own device via `LocalStorage`—no accounts or logins required.

## 🛠️ How to use

1. Open the tracker in your browser.
2. Filter by the specific biome you want to farm.
3. Click **"Visit In-Game"** to jump straight to the tile.
4. Set your custom timers for Farmlands or check off Ponds to keep your route perfectly organized.
